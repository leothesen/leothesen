Julian goes for a surf and I watch perfect peeling waves before having to join him for an hour over lunch. It’s small, quite clean and good. Waist high peeling rights that form on the shelf and run over the sandbar; almost like a more tropical Mdumbi. It’s not really big enough to let rip, but it’s always a pleasure to ride a long wave.

In the evening a British family arrive. They have a small, white and fluffy dog named Marley. Marley meets the three village dogs and is immediately in love. Well call it love or hormones as one of the three dogs is on heat. There’s some humping and suddenly the torches are out and British voices call for their dog. “Marley is off shagging the locals” the daughter remarks.
