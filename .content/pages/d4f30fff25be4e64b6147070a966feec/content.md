We quickly reach the carpark and have a chat with the person on duty. I really love the feeling of signing yourself back in on the mountain register. It’s both a sense of accomplishment and thrill. I think that the thrill comes from the fact that if we didn’t sign in, something quite serious would probably be going on. There are not many check points in life where you regard the fact that nothing went wrong in a positive light. It’s a little pat on the back that I try give myself as often as possible.
