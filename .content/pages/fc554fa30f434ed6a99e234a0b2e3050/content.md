It’s raining when I wake up, in a nostalgic warm kind of way. The way that only rain in a tropical rainforest can make you feel. Birds, frogs and drops lull me back to sleep. 
<empty-block/>
I take it easy and make some breakfast and do my goodbyes. I pop over to the ocean to see if it will entice me, but it’s stormy and quite small. I head over to Oribi gorge as per Samantha’s suggestion. 
<empty-block/>
Making a coffee on the rocks at the back of the river I meet Kgmotso. She is a newly ordained sangoma. She speak about her journey and how she used to be a “slay queen” in the third dimension. Now in the fifth dimension things are slightly more lovey, but she’s found her calling. Togozani with two claps - hands on top of eachother and kneeling - is the greeting for healers. We speak about Leo’s and how I am on this journey as I am a strong person.
