Sun. Suncream. Sunburn. Love to hate to love them. 
<empty-block/>
Turning thirty this year: within the body the feeling of impermanence is creeping in. I was inspired by some particularly diligent people - with beautifully healthy skin - to take better care of my own. So I put out this message to a few people in my life who I suspected used suncream on the daily: 
<empty-block/>
> Hello beautiful person in my life with beautiful skin 🪷
	Do you have any daily facial suncream recommendations for me? 🌞🧴
	All suggestions welcome - it would be amazing if you could write a little review of the suncream 📝<br>I've tried a few now, but many of them have been a bit too oily or shiny. 🛢️
	This is a bit of a broadcast message - and I will be compiling the results; respond with OPT IN if you would like to be notified when this survey is complete. 📊
	Leoxoxo
