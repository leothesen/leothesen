The Klein Karoo feels different to how I remember it. No longer suffocating, desolate and dead. No longer the absence of an ocean. The calm, yet vibrant stillness is intoxicating. I can’t keep my eyes off the horizon. 
<empty-block/>
Negative space is the key to appreciation here: three trees standing in an isolated cluster increase their presence and accentuate their features. Something that my younger self could never grasp when coming from the abundant coast. 
<empty-block/>
One thing has remained the same within me throughout these years - this is the best landscape for throwing rocks at said lonely trees.
