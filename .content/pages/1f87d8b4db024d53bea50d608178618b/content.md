# Mdumbi mush
## Ingredients 
- 7 salticrax 
- A lot of Spur salad and French fry dressing 
- Enough Pako curry paste 
- 2 boiled eggs 
- Some green beans
- 1 diced onion 
- Enough butter to coat a pan. Then double that. 
- More grated cheese than you think 
## Method
1. Boil the eggs for 10 minutes
2. Discard water and place eggs in cold water 
3. Melt butter 
4. Sauté onions until soft
5. Add curry paste
6. Add diced hard boiled eggs
7. Add cheese
8. Turn heat off
9. Add Spur sauce
